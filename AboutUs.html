About Us

Emperor Key is a property leasing and management firm that offers a full range of services for property owners and tenants. Our services include after-sales follow up for property investors, mortgage application, furniture purchase and installation, property leasing, management, relocation packages, cash management, and many others.

Our active markets for property leasing and management services are primarily in Thailand, including cities such as Bangkok, Phuket, Chiang Mai and Pattaya.

Many of our property owners are from overseas markets such as Hong Kong and China. With offices in Bangkok, Hong Kong and Shanghai, Emperor Key is able to provide a comprehensive one-stop shop and cross border service to our clients. Our international clients can deal with our international offices in their own home country without the need to incur costly international phone calls or to deal with language barrier.

Our Partners

In helping our clients to better manage their properties we partner with property developers with whom they bought their properties from. In the case of Thailand we work closely with Sansiri Public Company Limited and their property management subsidiary PLUS Property Co. Ltd.

Sansiri is one of the largest real estate developers in Thailand offering a full range of housing units; single-detached houses, semi-detached houses, townhouses, and condominiums. The Company remains the first and only Southeast Asian developer to have received the prestigious Commendation for Excellence Awards by Asia Pacific Commercial Real Estate (APCRE). Sansiri was founded in 1984 as the holding company to manage the assets of the Chutrakul family, before merging with the Lamsam family company in 1994. The Company has been listed on the Stock Exchange of Thailand since 1996. Sansiri is known for delivering high quality property projects to investors. This is evidenced by Sansiri picking up the prestigious Best Developer Thailand 2013 award. Acknowledging Sansiri’s success, the judging panel stated: “With 257 projects since 1984, Thailand’s Sansiri has introduced numerous innovations and advancements in the luxury villa and condominium sector, with customer satisfaction and lifestyle always at the core of the company’s impressive achievements.”

PLUS Property Co., Ltd., a subsidiary of Sansiri Public Company Limited, provides real estate brokerage and facility management services. It offers property agency, sales management, and consultancy services. Its facility management services include total facility management, engineering service management, corporate facility management, and engineering consultancy services.
